## KEY PATTERNS IN REAL RESULTS

1. **Portfolio approach beats single-product**: saas.group ($100M ARR from 25 products) >> single product risk
2. **Speed to market matters more than perfection**: Fireship built Vocalize.Cloud in 2 days
3. **Automation compounds**: Bot profits are 24/7 — even small edges become huge with time
4. **Airdrop farming has the craziest ROI** but highest variance and moral/legal gray area
5. **Local business automation is the sleeper hit**: $300-500/mo recurring × 20 clients = $6-10K/mo with minimal churn
6. **Nobody is making money from "AI content creation" at scale** — the real money is in tools/infrastructure, not content itself
