## Description
**⚡ The ultimate productivity toolkit for Shopify store owners.**

Stop clicking through endless pages. Shopify Power Tools adds inline editing, bulk operations, and smart analytics directly into your Shopify admin.

**🆓 FREE FEATURES:**
• **Inline Product Editor** — Double-click any field in your product list to edit in place. No more opening each product separately.
• **SEO Score Panel** — Instant SEO analysis on every product page: title length, meta descriptions, alt tags, URL handles. Get a letter grade (A-F) with specific fix suggestions.
• **Image Optimizer** — Automatic suggestions for image dimensions, file formats, and missing alt text.

**🚀 PRO FEATURES ($14.99/mo):**
• **Bulk Price Editor** — Increase or decrease prices by percentage or fixed amount across all products or selected collections. Preview before applying. Round to .99.
• **Bulk Inventory Updater** — Set, add, or subtract inventory across products in one click.
• **Competitor Price Monitor** — Track any Shopify store's product count and average pricing.
• **Order Dashboard Overlay** — Daily/weekly order trends with visual bar charts, right on your orders page.
• **Discount Code Generator** — Generate batches of discount codes with custom prefixes. Export to CSV.
• **Customer Lifetime Value** — See avg CLV, repeat rate, avg order value, and top 10 customers.
• **Abandoned Cart Recovery Stats** — Track abandoned carts, lost revenue, and recovery rates.

**Why store owners love it:**
✅ Works inside your existing Shopify admin — no new tabs or dashboards
✅ Double-click to edit anything
✅ Bulk operations save hours of manual work
✅ SEO scoring catches issues before they hurt rankings
✅ All data stays in your browser — we never see your store data

**Works on:** All Shopify plans (Basic, Shopify, Advanced, Plus)
