## Full Description

### 📊 The analytics dashboard eBay sellers have been waiting for.

Stop guessing your real profits. eBay Seller Analytics overlays powerful tools directly on Seller Hub — no separate app needed.

**FREE FEATURES:**

📈 **Sales Velocity Tracker**
See exactly how many items you're selling per day, week, and month. Trend arrows show if you're accelerating or slowing down.

💰 **Real Profit Calculator**
Know your ACTUAL profit on every listing. We factor in:
• eBay's 13.25% final value fee
• Payment processing fees (2.9% + $0.30)
• Your cost of goods (editable per item)
• Shipping costs
No more spreadsheet math!

⭐ **Listing Quality Score (A-F)**
Every active listing gets a quality grade based on:
• Title optimization (are you using all 80 characters?)
• Photo count (12 is the sweet spot)
• Description completeness
• Item specifics filled in
• Returns policy & free shipping
Actionable tips to improve each score.

📊 **Revenue Dashboard**
Beautiful 30-day charts showing revenue, profit, and sales trends — overlaid right on your Seller Hub overview.

Export all your analytics data to CSV for accounting or deeper analysis.
