**⚡ Tab Master Pro — The Ultimate Tab & Session Manager**

Drowning in tabs? Tab Master Pro gives you superpowers.

**🆓 FREE FEATURES:**

📁 **Save & Restore Sessions** — Save all your open tabs as a named session with one click. Restore them anytime. Never lose your workflow again.

🔍 **Instant Tab Search** — Fuzzy search across ALL open tabs in ALL windows. Find any tab in milliseconds.

💤 **Suspend Inactive Tabs** — Free up memory by suspending tabs you're not using. See exactly how much RAM you've saved.

🔄 **Duplicate Detector** — Find and close duplicate tabs automatically. One click cleanup.

📊 **Tab Timeline** — Visual history of every tab you've opened today. See your browsing patterns.

⌨️ **Keyboard Shortcuts** — Alt+S to save, Alt+F to search, Alt+M to suspend, Alt+D to dedupe.

🎨 **Auto-Group by Domain** — Automatically organize tabs into color-coded groups by website.

⏰ **Schedule Sessions** — Set sessions to open automatically at specific times. Perfect for daily workflows.

- Side panel support for persistent view

**Keyboard Shortcuts:**

Built for power users who live in their browser.
