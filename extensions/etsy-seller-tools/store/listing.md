### 🚀 The #1 Free Tool for Etsy Sellers

Stop guessing. Start growing. Etsy Seller Power Tools gives you the insights you need to optimize your listings, outsmart competitors, and increase sales — right inside your Etsy dashboard.

### ✨ FREE Features:

**🏷️ SEO Tag Analyzer**
Get an instant 0-100 score for every tag on your listing. See which tags are too long, too generic, or missing keywords — and get specific tips to improve each one.

**📊 Listing Quality Score**
Grade your listings across 5 key dimensions: Title, Photos, Tags, Description, and Shipping. Know exactly what to fix to rank higher in Etsy search.

**🕵️ Competitor Spy (3 lookups/day)**
Enter any shop name and see their bestsellers, pricing strategy, and review velocity. Learn what's working in your niche.

**⭐ Star Seller Progress Tracker**
Overlay on your dashboard showing real-time progress toward Star Seller status across all three metrics.

**💬 Review Response Templates**
One-click copy professional review responses. 5 built-in templates for every situation — thank yous, concerns, shipping delays, and more.

**📈 Sales Dashboard Overlay**
Enhanced analytics overlay on your Etsy dashboard with conversion rates, average order value, and trending listings.
