## Detailed Description
**Stop wasting hours on repetitive hosting tasks.** Airbnb Host Power Tools supercharges your Airbnb hosting dashboard with the features Airbnb should have built.

### 📨 Message Templates
Pre-written templates for check-in instructions, house rules, directions, and checkout reminders. Insert with one click — no more retyping the same message for every guest.

**Variables auto-fill:** Set your WiFi password, address, check-in time once → every template uses them automatically.

### ⭐ Review Response Templates  
Respond to guest reviews in seconds with professional, customizable templates. Positive, neutral, and constructive responses ready to go.

### 📊 Quick Stats Dashboard
See your key hosting metrics at a glance — messages sent, reviews responded, and more — overlaid right on your Airbnb dashboard.

### 💰 Pricing Analytics (Pro)
Compare your nightly rate against nearby listings. See area average, median, price range, and where you rank. Data-driven pricing decisions.

### 📅 Calendar Analysis (Pro)
Spot booking gaps, identify open weekends, and get actionable pricing suggestions to maximize occupancy.
