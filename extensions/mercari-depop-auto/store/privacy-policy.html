RelistPro — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

Overview

RelistPro is a browser extension that helps Mercari sellers relist items efficiently. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect (none) and how the extension works.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. RelistPro does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, browsing data, or usage data of any kind.

How It Works

  • All processing happens locally in your browser.
  • No data is sent to any external server.
  • No analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind.
  • No cookies or identifiers are created.
  • No accounts or sign-ups required.

Permissions

The extension requests only the minimum permissions needed to interact with Mercari pages in your browser. It does not access any other websites or data.

Third Parties

RelistPro does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. There are no ads.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
