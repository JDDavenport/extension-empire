## Description
**Stop wasting hours managing Facebook Marketplace listings manually.**

FB Marketplace Pro gives you the seller tools Facebook forgot to build:

🔄 **Auto-Relist Expired Listings** — One click to relist all expired/ended listings
📦 **Bulk Listing Creator** — Import items from CSV, auto-fill listing forms
💬 **Message Templates** — Quick-reply to buyers with saved templates (3 free, unlimited with Pro)
📉 **Price Drop Scheduler** — Auto-reduce price by X% after Y days
📊 **Competitor Price Scanner** — See average prices for similar items
📋 **Cross-Post Helper** — Copy listings formatted for Craigslist & OfferUp
✅ **Sales Tracker** — Track sold items, revenue & average days to sell
🔍 **Listing Quality Checker** — Get tips on photos, descriptions & pricing
⚡ **Bump/Renew All** — Refresh all listings with one click

### Free Features
- Sales tracker (last 10 items)

- 3 message templates

### Pro Features ($9.99/mo)
- Everything in Free
- Unlimited message templates

**Works directly on facebook.com/marketplace — no data leaves your browser.**

### How It Works
1. Install the extension
2. Go to Facebook Marketplace
3. Click the 🏪 floating button
4. Access all tools from the side panel

### CSV Format for Bulk Listings
