## Name
Amazon Seller Dashboard — Profit Calculator & Analytics

## Short Description (132 chars)
FBA profit calculator, sales tracker, inventory alerts, BSR history & competitor monitoring for Amazon sellers. All-in-one dashboard.

## Description
**The all-in-one dashboard for Amazon FBA & FBM sellers — right inside Seller Central.**

Stop switching between tabs and spreadsheets. Amazon Seller Dashboard gives you instant profit calculations, inventory alerts, and sales analytics as overlays directly on the pages you already use.

### 🆓 FREE Features
✅ **Profit Calculator** — See real-time profit breakdown on any product (FBA fees, referral fees, shipping, COGS)
✅ **Inventory Alerts** — Get warned about low stock and stranded inventory
✅ **Review Monitor** — New reviews highlighted automatically on product pages

### ⭐ PRO Features ($19.99/mo)
📈 **Sales Velocity Tracker** — Daily, weekly, and monthly sales trends with charts
📊 **BSR History** — Track Best Seller Rank over time with visual charts
🔍 **Competitor Tracker** — Monitor competitor price, BSR, and review changes
🔑 **Keyword Rank Tracker** — Track your keyword positions over time
💰 **PPC Campaign Stats** — Quick overview of campaign performance
📥 **CSV Export** — Export any data for your own analysis

### Why Sellers Love It
- **Zero setup** — Works instantly on Seller Central
- **Non-intrusive** — Clean overlay that doesn't break anything
- **Fast** — Lightweight, no slowdowns
- **Private** — All data stays in your browser, nothing sent to external servers
- **Dark theme** — Easy on the eyes during late-night inventory sessions

### How It Works
1. Install the extension
2. Visit Seller Central or any Amazon product page
3. Click the 📊 button to open your dashboard
4. Configure COGS and settings for accurate profit calculations

### Permissions
This extension only accesses sellercentral.amazon.com and amazon.com pages. No data is collected or transmitted externally.
