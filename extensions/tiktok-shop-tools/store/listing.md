## Description

### 📊 The #1 Toolkit for TikTok Shop Sellers

Stop switching between tabs. **TikTok Shop Seller Tools** puts everything you need in one powerful overlay — right on your seller dashboard.

TikTok Shop is the fastest-growing ecommerce platform in the US, and you need the right tools to stay ahead. Whether you're doing $100/day or $10,000/day, this extension saves you hours every week.

### ✨ Features

**📈 Real-Time Dashboard Overlay**
See your key metrics at a glance — revenue, orders, average order value, and conversion rate — all in a beautiful floating dashboard directly on seller-us.tiktok.com. No more digging through pages.

**💬 Quick Reply Templates**
Respond to buyers in seconds. Pre-save your most common replies (order status, return policy, thank you messages) and paste them with one click. Reply faster = better reviews = more sales.

**🔔 Inventory Alerts (Pro)**
Never run out of stock again. Set custom thresholds and get browser notifications when products are running low. Low-stock items are highlighted in red so you can reorder fast.

**📥 Order Export to CSV (Pro)**
Export your orders to CSV with one click. Perfect for bookkeeping, tax prep, or importing into your inventory management system.

**🏷️ Competitor Price Tracking (Pro)**
Know what your competitors are charging. Stay competitive without constantly checking other listings.
