## Design Notes:
- Color palette: Navy (#1B2A4A), Gold (#D4A853), White (#FFFFFF), Light Gray (#F5F5F5)
- Font: Clean sans-serif (Montserrat or similar)
- NO clipart — use geometric shapes, lines, minimal icons
- Target aesthetic: "premium business tool" not "Etsy craft"
- All images 2000x2000px or 2000x1600px for landscape
