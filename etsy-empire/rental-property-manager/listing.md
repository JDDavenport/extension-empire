**🏠 Complete Rental Property Management Spreadsheet — For Landlords with 1-20 Units**

Finally, a spreadsheet that does what expensive property management software does — **without the monthly fees**. Track tenants, collect rent, log maintenance, monitor expenses, and see your profit per property — all in one powerful Google Sheets template.

Built by real estate investors for landlords and property managers who want **complete financial visibility** without the complexity of Buildium, AppFolio, or Stessa.

**📋 8 CONNECTED SHEETS INCLUDED:**

**1. 🏢 Property Portfolio**
Your birds-eye view of all properties:
- Property address, type (SFH, duplex, condo, apartment), unit count
- Purchase price, current value, mortgage balance
- Monthly mortgage payment (PITI breakdown)
- Equity position calculated automatically
- Total portfolio value and equity at a glance

**2. 👥 Tenant Directory**
Everything about every tenant:
- Tenant name, contact info (phone, email, emergency contact)
- Property and unit assignment
- Lease start/end dates with **automatic expiration alerts** (30, 60, 90 days)
- Monthly rent amount, security deposit held
- Lease status: Active, Month-to-Month, Notice Given, Vacant
- **Color-coded:** Red = lease expiring within 30 days, Yellow = 60 days, Green = active

**3. 💰 Rent Roll**
Track every dollar:
- Monthly grid: each tenant, amount due, amount paid, date paid, balance
- **Automatic late detection** — highlights unpaid rent after due date
- Running balance per tenant
- Monthly and annual income totals
- Vacancy tracking with lost income calculation
- Payment method tracking (check, Venmo, Zelle, direct deposit)

**4. 🔧 Maintenance Log**
Never lose track of repairs:
- Date reported, property, unit, description
- Priority: Emergency / Urgent / Routine / Cosmetic
- Status: Reported → Scheduled → In Progress → Completed
- Vendor/contractor assigned
- Cost (estimated vs. actual)
- Warranty status
- Annual maintenance cost per property

**5. 💸 Expense Tracker**
Every expense categorized for tax time:
- Date, property, category, vendor, amount, payment method
- **Tax-ready categories:** Mortgage interest, property tax, insurance, repairs, maintenance, utilities, management fees, advertising, legal, travel, supplies, depreciation
- Monthly and annual totals per property
- Per-category summaries (your CPA will love you)

See exactly how each property performs:
- **Income:** Rent, late fees, pet fees, laundry, parking, other
- **Expenses:** Automatically pulled from Expense Tracker
- **Net Operating Income (NOI)** calculated automatically
- **Cash flow** (after mortgage) per property per month
- **Cap rate, cash-on-cash return, and GRM** calculated
- Year-over-year comparison columns

**7. 📅 Lease Expiration Calendar**
Visual timeline of all leases:
- Sorted by expiration date
- Days remaining calculated automatically
- Renewal status tracking (Renewed, In Negotiation, Not Started, Vacating)
- Rent increase notes
