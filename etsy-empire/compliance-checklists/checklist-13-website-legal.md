# ☑️ Website Legal Compliance Checklist

---

### REQUIRED LEGAL PAGES
- [ ] **Privacy Policy** — Required by law if you collect ANY personal data (including analytics cookies)
  - What data you collect and how
  - Third-party services (Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, email providers)
  - Cookie usage and types
  - User rights and opt-out options
  - Contact information
- [ ] **Terms of Service / Terms of Use** — Governs use of your website
  - Limitation of liability
  - Intellectual property ownership
  - User conduct rules
  - Dispute resolution (arbitration clause, governing law)
  - Account termination rights
- [ ] **Cookie Policy** (can be part of Privacy Policy or separate)
  - Types of cookies used (necessary, analytics, marketing)
  - Third-party cookies
  - How to manage/delete cookies

### ACCESSIBILITY (ADA/WCAG)
- [ ] **WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance** — Increasingly enforced, especially for businesses open to the public
  - Alt text on all images
  - Proper heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3)
  - Sufficient color contrast (4.5:1 for normal text)
  - Keyboard navigation works for all functionality
  - Form labels and error messages accessible
  - Video captions/transcripts provided
  - Links have descriptive text (not "click here")
  - Font size adjustable
  - No content that flashes more than 3 times per second
- [ ] **Test with accessibility tools** — WAVE, Lighthouse, axe DevTools
- [ ] **Screen reader tested** — NVDA (free), VoiceOver (Mac), JAWS
- [ ] **Accessibility statement** published (recommended but not always required)

### E-COMMERCE SPECIFIC
- [ ] Return/refund policy clearly posted
- [ ] Shipping policy with timelines
- [ ] Pricing displayed accurately (including taxes/fees)
- [ ] Product descriptions accurate and not misleading
- [ ] Customer reviews not fake or manipulated (FTC guidelines)
- [ ] Endorsement disclosures (#ad, #sponsored — FTC Act)
- [ ] Subscription terms clearly disclosed (auto-renewal laws)
- [ ] Cancellation process clearly available
- [ ] Payment security badges/trust indicators

### EMAIL COMPLIANCE
- [ ] CAN-SPAM compliant (see Data Privacy Checklist #12)
- [ ] Double opt-in for email marketing (best practice, required by GDPR)
- [ ] Unsubscribe link in every marketing email
- [ ] Physical address in email footer
- [ ] Email list hygiene — remove bounces and unsubscribes promptly

### COPPA (Children's Privacy — If Applicable)
- [ ] If your site is directed at or knowingly collects data from children under 13:
  - Verifiable parental consent before collecting data
  - Clear privacy policy re: children's data
  - Parents can review, delete, and refuse further collection

### CONTENT & IP
- [ ] All images properly licensed (stock photos, Creative Commons, original)
- [ ] No copyrighted content used without permission
- [ ] DMCA takedown process available (if user-generated content)
- [ ] Trademark symbols used correctly
- [ ] Testimonials are genuine and properly disclosed
- [ ] Affiliate links properly disclosed (FTC)

### TECHNICAL SECURITY
- [ ] SSL/HTTPS enabled (non-negotiable)
- [ ] Regular software/plugin updates
- [ ] Strong admin passwords + 2FA
- [ ] Regular backups
- [ ] Security monitoring/scanning
- [ ] Forms protected against spam (CAPTCHA)

---

**⚠️ ADA website lawsuits are increasing rapidly. Proactive compliance is much cheaper than litigation.**
