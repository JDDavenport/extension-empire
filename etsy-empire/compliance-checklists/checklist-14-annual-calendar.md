# ☑️ Annual Compliance Calendar

---

## JANUARY
- [ ] Issue W-2s to employees (due Jan 31)
- [ ] Issue 1099-NEC to contractors (due Jan 31)
- [ ] File Form 940 (FUTA annual return) — Jan 31
- [ ] File Q4 payroll taxes (Form 941) — Jan 31
- [ ] Fourth quarter estimated tax payment — Jan 15
- [ ] Post OSHA 300A summary (Feb 1 - April 30)
- [ ] Review and update employee handbook

## FEBRUARY
- [ ] OSHA 300A summary posted (through April 30)
- [ ] File W-2s/W-3 with SSA (if not already done)
- [ ] File 1099s with IRS (Feb 28 paper / March 31 electronic)
- [ ] Review business insurance policies for renewal

## MARCH
- [ ] Partnership/S-Corp tax returns due — March 15 (or file extension)
- [ ] K-1s issued to partners/shareholders
- [ ] First quarter estimated tax planning
- [ ] Review Q1 financial performance

## APRIL
- [ ] Individual/C-Corp tax returns due — April 15 (or file extension)
- [ ] First quarter estimated tax payment — April 15
- [ ] File Q1 payroll taxes (Form 941) — April 30
- [ ] Take down OSHA 300A summary (April 30)
- [ ] Spring business license renewals (check your dates)
- [ ] Review and update business continuity plan

## MAY
- [ ] Review workers' comp policy (many renew mid-year)
- [ ] Mid-year financial review and budget check
- [ ] Employee performance reviews (if semi-annual)

## JUNE
- [ ] Second quarter estimated tax payment — June 15
- [ ] Mid-year tax planning review with CPA
- [ ] Review and update data privacy practices
- [ ] Cybersecurity assessment

## JULY
- [ ] File Q2 payroll taxes (Form 941) — July 31
- [ ] Review business goals — halfway point check
- [ ] Update employee training records
- [ ] Review vendor contracts for renewal

## AUGUST
- [ ] Begin planning for year-end
- [ ] Review health insurance options for open enrollment
- [ ] Update business asset inventory for insurance

## SEPTEMBER
- [ ] Third quarter estimated tax payment — September 15
- [ ] Extended Partnership/S-Corp returns due — September 15
- [ ] Review retirement plan contributions (SEP-IRA, 401k)
- [ ] Open enrollment planning for health benefits

## OCTOBER
- [ ] Extended Individual/C-Corp returns due — October 15
- [ ] File Q3 payroll taxes (Form 941) — October 31
- [ ] Open enrollment for health insurance
- [ ] Prepare for year-end bookkeeping

## NOVEMBER
- [ ] Year-end tax planning (defer income? accelerate expenses?)
- [ ] Review and update all compliance checklists
- [ ] Plan for 1099/W-2 preparation
- [ ] Review state annual report due dates
- [ ] Employee performance reviews (if annual)

## DECEMBER
- [ ] Year-end financial close preparation
- [ ] Maximize retirement contributions before Dec 31
- [ ] Use remaining FSA/HSA funds
- [ ] Review and renew business licenses
- [ ] Update compliance calendar for next year
- [ ] Holiday bonus/gift tax considerations
- [ ] Inventory count (if applicable)

---

### RECURRING (Monthly)
- [ ] Reconcile bank/credit card statements
- [ ] Review P&L and cash flow
- [ ] Pay sales tax (if monthly filer)
- [ ] Back up financial and business data
- [ ] Review accounts receivable/payable

### RECURRING (Quarterly)
- [ ] File and pay estimated taxes
- [ ] File payroll tax returns (Form 941)
- [ ] Review insurance coverage
- [ ] Self-audit compliance status
- [ ] File sales tax (if quarterly filer)
