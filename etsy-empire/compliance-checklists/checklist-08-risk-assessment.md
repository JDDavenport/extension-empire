# ☑️ Risk Assessment Checklist

---

### OPERATIONAL RISKS
- [ ] Single points of failure identified (key employees, critical vendors, single revenue source)
- [ ] Supply chain risks assessed (backup suppliers identified)
- [ ] Equipment failure contingency plan in place
- [ ] Business continuity plan documented
- [ ] Disaster recovery plan (fire, flood, natural disaster)
- [ ] Quality control processes documented

### FINANCIAL RISKS
- [ ] Cash flow projections maintained (minimum 90 days forward)
- [ ] Emergency fund established (3-6 months of operating expenses)
- [ ] Customer concentration risk assessed (no single customer >25% of revenue)
- [ ] Payment terms and collections process defined
- [ ] Credit risk assessment for large accounts
- [ ] Fraud prevention controls in place (dual signatures, expense approvals)

### LEGAL & COMPLIANCE RISKS
- [ ] Contracts reviewed by attorney
- [ ] Intellectual property protected (trademarks, copyrights, patents, NDAs)
- [ ] Employment law compliance verified
- [ ] Industry-specific regulations identified and addressed
- [ ] Data privacy obligations met
- [ ] Liability insurance adequate for risk exposure

### CYBERSECURITY RISKS
- [ ] Strong password policy enforced
- [ ] Multi-factor authentication enabled on all business accounts
- [ ] Data backup system in place (3-2-1 rule: 3 copies, 2 media types, 1 offsite)
- [ ] Antivirus/endpoint protection on all devices
- [ ] Employee cybersecurity training completed
- [ ] Incident response plan documented
- [ ] Vendor security assessed for critical service providers
- [ ] Customer data encryption in place

### REPUTATION RISKS
- [ ] Online review monitoring system in place
- [ ] Social media policy for employees
- [ ] Crisis communication plan documented
- [ ] Customer complaint resolution process defined
- [ ] Brand monitoring (Google Alerts at minimum)
