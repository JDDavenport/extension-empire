# ☑️ Business License & Permit Checklist

---

### FEDERAL LICENSES
- [ ] Research if your industry requires a federal license (TTB for alcohol, ATF for firearms, FCC for broadcasting, USDA for agriculture, DOT for transportation, EPA for environmental)
- [ ] Apply for applicable federal licenses/permits
- [ ] Note renewal dates in your compliance calendar

### STATE LICENSES
- [ ] General state business license/registration
- [ ] State professional license (if applicable: contractors, real estate, healthcare, cosmetology, accounting, legal, etc.)
- [ ] State sales tax permit (if selling taxable goods/services)
- [ ] State employer registration (if hiring employees)
- [ ] State-specific industry permits (food service, childcare, auto dealer, etc.)
- [ ] Home-based business registration (if applicable)

### LOCAL (CITY/COUNTY) LICENSES
- [ ] City business license/tax registration
- [ ] County business license (if outside city limits or county requires)
- [ ] Zoning compliance verification — Is your business location zoned for your use?
- [ ] Sign permit (for exterior business signage)
- [ ] Building permit (for renovations/buildout)
- [ ] Occupancy permit / Certificate of Occupancy
- [ ] Fire department inspection/permit
- [ ] Health department permit (food, beverage, personal services)
- [ ] Alarm permit (if security system installed)

### SPECIALTY PERMITS
- [ ] Seller's permit / resale certificate
- [ ] Environmental permits (waste, emissions, water discharge)
- [ ] Liquor license (if serving/selling alcohol)
- [ ] Music/entertainment license (ASCAP, BMI, SESAC for playing music)
- [ ] Food handler's certification (for all food service employees)
- [ ] Weights and measures certification (if selling by weight)
