# ☑️ Employee Handbook Essentials Checklist

---

### INTRODUCTION
- [ ] Welcome statement / company mission and values
- [ ] At-will employment disclaimer (with acknowledgment signature page)
- [ ] Handbook purpose and limitations (not a contract)
- [ ] Right to modify disclaimer

### EMPLOYMENT POLICIES
- [ ] Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) statement
- [ ] Anti-harassment and anti-discrimination policy (including reporting procedure)
- [ ] Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) accommodation policy
- [ ] Immigration law compliance
- [ ] Employment classifications (full-time, part-time, temporary, exempt, non-exempt)
- [ ] Background check policy (if applicable)
- [ ] Introductory/probationary period (if applicable)
- [ ] Internal transfer/promotion policy

### COMPENSATION & BENEFITS
- [ ] Pay periods and payday schedule
- [ ] Overtime policy
- [ ] Timekeeping requirements
- [ ] Direct deposit information
- [ ] Benefits overview (health, dental, vision, retirement)
- [ ] Benefits eligibility and enrollment periods
- [ ] Workers' compensation information

### TIME OFF & LEAVE
- [ ] Paid time off (PTO) / Vacation policy (accrual, carryover, payout)
- [ ] Sick leave policy (check state/city requirements)
- [ ] Holiday schedule
- [ ] FMLA policy (if 50+ employees)
- [ ] State family/medical leave (if applicable)
- [ ] Bereavement leave
- [ ] Jury duty leave
- [ ] Military leave (USERRA)
- [ ] Voting leave
- [ ] Personal leave of absence

### WORKPLACE CONDUCT
- [ ] Code of conduct / professional behavior expectations
- [ ] Attendance and punctuality
- [ ] Dress code / appearance standards
- [ ] Drug and alcohol policy (including drug testing if applicable)
- [ ] Workplace violence prevention
- [ ] Conflict of interest policy
- [ ] Outside employment policy
- [ ] Confidentiality / non-disclosure expectations
- [ ] Company property use policy

### TECHNOLOGY & COMMUNICATION
- [ ] Email and internet use policy
- [ ] Social media policy
- [ ] Cell phone / personal device policy
- [ ] Company equipment policy
- [ ] Electronic monitoring disclosure (if applicable)
- [ ] Data security and privacy obligations
- [ ] Remote work / telecommuting policy (if applicable)

### SAFETY & SECURITY
- [ ] Workplace safety commitment
- [ ] Reporting injuries and accidents
- [ ] Emergency procedures reference
- [ ] Building security and access
- [ ] Visitor policy

### DISCIPLINE & TERMINATION
- [ ] Progressive discipline policy (verbal warning → written → final → termination)
  - Note: Preserve at-will status — don't guarantee progressive steps
- [ ] Grounds for immediate termination
- [ ] Resignation procedures and notice expectations
- [ ] Return of company property
- [ ] Final paycheck information
- [ ] Exit interview (optional)

### ACKNOWLEDGMENT
- [ ] **Handbook acknowledgment form** — Employee signs confirming receipt, understanding, at-will status
  - Keep signed copy in personnel file
  - ⚠️ CRITICAL: Always get this signed

---

**⚠️ Have an employment attorney review your handbook for state-specific requirements before distribution.**
